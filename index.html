Why GloFi?

Capitally Efficient

Why let your assets sit stagnantly? Borrowing against something you plan to HODL is a great way to keep exposure while unlocking assets to invest in other plays you’re bullish on.

Low Risk

The protocol does not want you to get liquidated. Auto compounding of collateral on certain asset types constantly improves loan health. Several other features make gLoop one of the safest places to loop.

Secure

Extremely talented developers with smart contract auditing experience developed glofi. We will also have top auditing firms go through all of the contracts before going live.

Fun

The Glofi community is made up of awesome ethereum loving individuals who are passionate about web3 and the future of finance.
Three Token System &Auto Compounding
Three Token System
Gloop is a borrowing protocol with three tokens. IOU is an accounting token for debt, Gloop is a utility token, and GDC is a stablecoin backed by assets brought in by borrowers.
Auto Compounding
As you earn yield on Gloop, most of it is taken and used to increase your collateral to constantly leverage you down.  This allows our users to constantly feel safer as they allow their wealth to grow.
Built to Boost
Glofi allows multiple collateral types with more being considered for the future. Borrow stablecoins against ethereum tokens you are super bullish on like GLP.
How it works?
You can deposit yield bearing tokens as collateral and earn yield on them while borrowing against their value. Over time, the yield earned on your initial deposit can pay off your loan.
Connect Wallet
Connect your wallet with the “connect wallet” button on the top right side of the page.
Find your Vault &Deposit
Select your favorite vault in the vault’s tab above to deposit the collateral of your choice. Only one position can be opened per wallet.
Acquire GDC
After depositing collateral, you will be issued IOU tokens which always equal $1 but can not leave the system. Exchange these for $GDC, GloFi Dollar Coin, and consider staking some IOU in a stability pool.
Convert your GDC to your favorite coin
You can visit the GDC curve pool to swap into your favorite tokens. Once you do some swaps, you could theoretically buy more collateral and leverage up!
